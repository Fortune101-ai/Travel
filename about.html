Discover the story behind our passion for creating extraordinary travel experiences and connecting people with the world's most amazing destinations.

Our Story

Founded in 1999, TravBloom began as a small travel agency with a big dream: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started as a passion project by a group of travel enthusiasts has grown into a leading travel company specializing in South African and Southern African destinations.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of travelers discover the beauty, culture, and adventure that South Africa has to offer. From the bustling streets of Cape Town to the serene beaches of Durban, from the spiritual temples to the breathtaking landscapes of neighboring countries, we've been there every step of the way.

Our Mission

At TravBloom, our mission is to create transformative travel experiences that connect people with the rich cultures, stunning landscapes, and vibrant communities of South Africa and Southern Africa.

We believe that travel has the power to broaden perspectives, create lasting memories, and foster understanding between different cultures. Through our carefully curated experiences, we aim to:

  • Showcase the authentic beauty and diversity of Southern Africa
  • Support local communities through responsible tourism practices
  • Create meaningful connections between travelers and local cultures
  • Preserve natural environments for future generations
  • Provide exceptional service that exceeds expectations
